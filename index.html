The Myanmar Cosplay Society (MCS) is the premier community for cosplayers, anime enthusiasts, and pop culture fans across Myanmar. Since our establishment, we've been dedicated to fostering creativity, friendship, and artistic expression through the art of cosplay.

Our community spans from Yangon to Mandalay and beyond, bringing together passionate individuals who love anime, manga, gaming, and character portrayal. Whether you're crafting your first costume or you're a veteran cosplayer, MCS provides a supportive environment for everyone to grow and shine.
